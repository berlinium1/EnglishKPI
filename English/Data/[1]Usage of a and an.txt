%Usage of a/an
"A" is used before words that start with consonant sounds 
("a sample" or "a model"), even if the consonant sound is made by 
a vowel ("a unit"). Conversely, "an" is used before words that start 
with vowel sounds ("an equation" or "an element"), 
even if the word starts with a silent consonant ("an hour").
